Playgroup (2-3 years)

Our playgroup program focuses on sensory exploration, social skills, and early communication through guided play and creative activities.

Nursery (3-4 years)

Nursery students engage in theme-based learning that encourages curiosity, problem-solving, and cooperation with peers.

Kindergarten (4-6 years)

Our kindergarten program prepares children for elementary school with a balanced approach of structured learning and creative play.

Elementary (6-10 years)

Elementary students benefit from our project-based curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.
